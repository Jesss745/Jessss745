Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

I've created 2 visuals to present this information. The sales over time by genre and by platform. I had filtered both data set to exclude data that had the "N/A" listed for the year to avoid skewing the data set.

For the genre, I went with an area chart.

This chart allowed me to see when the first games of each genre’s were created. For example, I found out that the first game in the Role-playing genre was released in 1986.
The most units sold from a specific genre in a year was was from games released in 2008 for the Action genre. On the other hand, Strategy seems to be the least popular genre overall.

For the sales over time by platform portion, I went with an line chart and a circles chart to indicate how much sales happened within the year.

So to start, I noticed that the PC is the longest lasting platform in this list with games still being created for it today. This might also be due to dataset not including games with the “N/A” years, but the TG16 only had games released for it for 1994, making it the shortest lasting platform.
The Wii had the best selling year on 2006, indicated by the size or the circle.
It’s also cool to know that the 2600 is the oldest platform on this list while XOne and PS4 is the newest consoles (released in 2013).
