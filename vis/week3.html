Visualization 4: All About Nintendo

Now for the highlight of this task, I decided to do a general analysis of Nintendo (the game publisher) as a whole!
I've always been a huge fan of all of their games, and they're really the only company where I actually somewhat keep up with their releases. As such, I decided this is a cool opportunity to just be a bit nerdy and talk about them :)

So, I'm sure we've all heard about Nintendo. Maybe even played a game or two from them. But, did you know how dominant they are in the video game industry when it comes to their sales number?
Feel free to hover over the pie chart to see the names, genres, and units sold for that game.

You can't say they're not doing well either. They have so many games that have sold more than 10 million units!
Its crazy becayse Nintendo has more games that have sold 10 million units compared to the the other 5 publishers in this data COMBINED!

Nintendo is unique compared to other publishers because they also make their own consoles and primarily only create games that are usually (not always!) only playble on those specific consoles (unless you pirate them perhaps...).

Nintendo definitely has a favorite genre though, and it sells really well!
