It's me, Jess!

I'm an interdisciplinary UX designer based in Vancouver whose passionate about bringing my ideas and product into reality through my experince creating illustrations and design. I take inspiration from all my past experiences and the skillsets I've developed through my time as a student at Simon Fraser University studying Interactive Arts and Technology where they've taught me various skills from creating 3D product mockups to coding an interactive game to help with learning.

Outside of school, I'm a huge foodie who loves to try new cuisine and rating them in my own personal journal to share with family and friends. I also love coming up with new illustrations of characters I enjoy from various media I consume.
